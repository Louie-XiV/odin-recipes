Southern Baked Macaroni & Cheese

Description

A rich, creamy baked mac & cheese recipe made with eggs, milk, sour cream, and double the cheddar. Lightened-up option included.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz uncooked elbow macaroni cook according to package directions
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 8 oz Cabot extra-sharp cheddar shredded
  • 12 oz Cabot's sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups milk whole milk for 2% for a lighter version
  • 4 tablespoon butter melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper optional
  • Salt and pepper to taste I use about ¼ teaspoon pepper and ½ teaspoon salt

Prep

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Boil macaroni al dente according to package directions, drain and cool.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all other ingredients (except 1 cup of cheddar cheese for topping).
  3. Toss with cooked macaroni.
  4. Transfer to a baking dish sprayed with olive oil cooking spray. Top with remaining cheese.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until just cheese just starts to turn golden (do not brown).
  6. Let sit for 10 minutes before serving to thicken.
